---
title: STAR Method for Behavioral Interview Answers
source: https://careers.microsoft.com/v2/global/en/hiring-tips/interview-tips.html
---

Use a specific example rather than a general claim. Briefly establish the
Situation and Task, spend most of the answer on the Actions you personally
took, and close with the Result. Results can include measurements, feedback,
what changed, or what you learned.

Keep the background short enough that the interviewer can follow the decision
you made. Use clear "I" statements for your work while still recognizing the
team's contribution.
