---
title: Entry-Level Technical Interview Preparation
source: https://learn.microsoft.com/training/career-paths/
---

Strong technical answers explain the problem before the solution. Describe
the chosen approach, one alternative or tradeoff, how correctness was tested,
and what happened after implementation. Interviewers need to understand the
reasoning, not only the final technology name.

When the candidate does not know an answer, they should clarify assumptions,
state what they know, and work through a structured investigation rather than
guessing.
