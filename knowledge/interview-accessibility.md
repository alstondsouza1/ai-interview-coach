---
title: Inclusive and Accessible Interview Practice
source: https://www.microsoft.com/diversity/inside-microsoft/cross-disability/accessibility
---

Interview practice should support different communication styles and access
needs. Useful options include adjustable timers, written questions, keyboard
navigation, readable contrast, and the ability to finish an answer after a
timer expires.

Timing should be treated as pacing feedback rather than a measure of candidate
ability. Users should be able to request accommodations in real interviews.
